Help Save Our Oceans

Somewhere in the world, a plastic bag is used, and most of the time, lost or thrown away

These bags are then carried away be the wind.

According to Waste Management, only 1 percent of plastic bags are returned for recycling.

Most of these bags are brought by the wind to the ocean.

Sea creatures mistake the platic for food.

100,000 marine creatures a year die from plastic entanglement and these are only the ones found.

This means that every plastic bag that is littered has a chance of killing an innocent creature.

Necessary Facts

  • There are believed to be 46,000 pieces of plastic in every square mile of ocean.
  • World wide, 13,000-15,000 pieces of plastic are dumped into the ocean every day.
